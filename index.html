About Us

Computer Science Community Club is a scientific club at USDB that shares knowledge in the computer science field through its various activities. Check our departments for more information.

YOU CAN MAKE THE DIFFERENCE! At CSCC, we believe in the transformative power of every individual. Each member holds the potential to shape the future of technology, innovation, and community impact. We're not just a club; we're a dynamic collective of enthusiasts, dreamers, and innovators.

Mission

  • Foster a collaborative environment for students interested in scientific applications of computer science.
  • Explore and engage in projects that leverage CS to solve real-world scientific problems.
  • Facilitate knowledge-sharing through workshops, seminars, and discussions.
  • Encourage innovation and creativity in scientific computing.

Get Involved

Join us for our regular meetings and events where we discuss new ideas, work on projects, and learn together. Stay tuned for announcements and updates on our schedule.

Projects

We actively work on various projects that involve scientific computing, ranging from data analysis to simulations and beyond. Explore our GitHub repositories to contribute or start your own project!

How to Contribute

  1. Fork the repository.
  2. Clone the forked repository to your local machine.
  3. Create a new branch for your feature or fix.
  4. Commit changes to your branch.
  5. Push your changes back to your fork.
  6. Submit a Pull Request from your forked repository to the main repository.

Please ensure to follow our code of conduct while contributing.
